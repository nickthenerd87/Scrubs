About Scrubs App

The SCRUBS app was created by Helathcare Workers And App Designers to help improve communication in a healthcare setting. We kept in mind the daily struggles for healthcare professionals everywhere. Medical Emergencies happen and every second counts Which is why we created a seamless communication app.

Feel confident in knowing that all messages are secure and cannot be screenshoted. Send Stat alerts to everyone in your unit who is online. You can also view your assignments and can view the assignments of other rooms on your unit so there is no confusion on who is providing care to all your patients.
